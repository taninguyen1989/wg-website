# AI OCR Integration: A Breakthrough in Data Entry

**AI OCR (Optical Character Recognition)** is changing how businesses handle input data.

## The Problem with Manual Entry
- Time-consuming.
- Prone to errors.
- High personnel costs.

## AI OCR Solution
With advanced AI technology, the system can:
1. **Automatically Recognize Invoice Patterns**: No manual template definition needed.
2. **Extract Information Accurately**: Reads even blurred or wrinkled invoices.
3. **Cross-check Information**: Automatically matches with POs and Goods Receipts.

> "AI OCR reduces data entry time by 90% and increases accuracy to 99.8%."
