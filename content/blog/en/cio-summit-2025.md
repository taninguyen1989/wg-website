# The Future of ECM and AI at CIO Summit 2025

The **CIO Summit 2025** brought fresh and breakthrough perspectives on the future of Enterprise Content Management (ECM) in the era of Artificial Intelligence.

## The AI Revolution in Document Management

At the conference, leading experts discussed the deep integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) into ECM systems. AI has moved beyond simple Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to semantic understanding, automatic classification, and intelligent information extraction.

## DocuWare: Automating 90% of Data Entry

A key highlight was the powerful integration capability of **DocuWare** with advanced AI technologies.

> "Combining DocuWare with AI Learning has helped automate up to 90% of manual data entry processes, minimizing errors to near zero." - shared a speaker at CIO Summit 2025.

### Key Benefits:
1. **Superior Processing Speed**: Reducing document processing time from days to minutes.
2. **Absolute Accuracy**: AI continuously learns from new document templates.
3. **Resource Liberation**: Personnel can focus on higher-value tasks.

## Conclusion

CIO Summit 2025 confirms that the combination of a solid ECM platform like DocuWare and the power of AI is the key for enterprises to breakout in the digital future.
