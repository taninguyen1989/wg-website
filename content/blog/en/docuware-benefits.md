# Why Enterprises Need Digital Transformation with DocuWare?

In the digital age, manual document management is becoming a major barrier to business growth. **DocuWare ECM** provides a comprehensive solution to address this issue.

## 1. Completely Eliminate Paperwork
DocuWare helps digitize 100% of paper documents, storing them centrally on a secure cloud platform. You can access information from anywhere, anytime.

## 2. Workflow Automation
No more manual file handovers. Processes such as **Invoice Approval**, **Contract Management**, or **Recruitment** are fully automated.
- Reduce processing time by 70%.
- Eliminate human errors.
- Transparency in work status.

## 3. Security and Compliance
With SOC 2 certification and GDPR compliance, your data is always protected at the highest level.
